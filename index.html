Philosophy

In his endless search for truth man is condemned to knowledge - everything else is whim. But when man is happy, the meaning of life and other eternal themes rarely interest him.

So, are we concerned with happiness or the eternal?

The preservation of simple human happiness requires mystery. Namely, the mysteries of pleasure, death, and love. To think about it is to know the day of ones death. Not knowing that day makes us practically immortal.
